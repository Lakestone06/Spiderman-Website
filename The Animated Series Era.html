📺 The Animated Series Era

🕷️ Spider-Man (1967)

As the very first television adaptation of the web-slinger, this classic 1967 animated series introduced Spider-Man to the small screen and gifted pop culture its most iconic, catchy theme song. Produced on a tight budget, the show relied on heavily recycled animation but successfully brought Peter Parker’s earliest comic book battles against villains like Green Goblin and Mysterio to life, creating timeless internet memes in the process.

👤 Voice Actor: Paul Soles

📅 Era: 1967 - 1970

🎥 Show: Spider-Man (Animated)

🎬 Key Producer: Stan Lee / Steve Krantz

🕷️ Spider-Man (1981)

Launching alongside a sister show in 1981, this short-lived solo animated series stayed incredibly faithful to the classic 1960s comic book art style of John Romita Sr. The show follows college student Peter Parker balancing university life with crime-fighting, defending New York City against traditional adversaries like Doctor Octopus, Kingpin, and Doctor Doom.

👤 Voice Actor: Ted Schwartz

📅 Era: 1981 - 1982

🎥 Show: Spider-Man (1981)

🎬 Key Director: Don Jurwich

🕷️ Spider-Man and His Amazing Friends (1981)

This beloved 1980s cartoon shifted the dynamic by pairing Peter Parker up with mutant heroes Iceman and Firestar to form the "Spider-Friends". Operating out of a high-tech secret lab inside Aunt May’s house, the trio regularly teamed up to defend the Earth from cosmic and mystical Marvel threats, making it a foundational crossover show for the broader animated Marvel Universe.

👤 Voice Actor: Dan Gilvezan

📅 Era: 1981 - 1983

🎥 Show: Spider-Man and His Amazing Friends

🎬 Key Director: Don Jurwich / Bob Richardson

🕷️ Spider-Man Unlimited (1999)

Serving as a wild, futuristic spin-off, this 1999 series saw Peter Parker travel to a dystopian alternate reality called Counter-Earth to rescue John Jameson. Equipped with a high-tech nanotechnology suit featuring a built-in web cape, Spider-Man leads a human rebellion against the High Evolutionary and his army of beast-men.

👤 Voice Actor: Rino Romano

📅 Era: 1999 - 2001

🎥 Show: Spider-Man Unlimited

🎬 Key Director: Patrick Archibald

🕷️ Spider-Man: The New Animated Series (2003)

Originally broadcast on MTV, this unique 2003 series used cutting-edge 3D cell-shaded animation and served as a loose, darker continuation of Sam Raimi's 2002 movie. The show follows a college-aged Peter Parker alongside Mary Jane and Harry Osborn at Empire State University, dealing with mature themes, high-tech corporate conspiracies, and slick, matrix-style action sequences.

👤 Voice Actor: Neil Patrick Harris

📅 Era: 2003

🎥 Show: Spider-Man: The New Animated Series

🎬 Executive Producer: Brian Michael Bendis

🏫 The Spectacular Spider-Man (2008)

Hailed by many critics and fans as the absolute best Spider-Man show ever created, this 2008 masterpiece perfectly blended high school drama with high-stakes heroics. The series captured the precise emotional core of Peter Parker's dual life, featuring fluid, stylized action choreography and masterfully plotted character webs that evolved beautifully over its two seasons.

👤 Voice Actor: Josh Keaton

📅 Era: 2008 - 2009

🎥 Show: The Spectacular Spider-Man

🎬 Showrunner: Greg Weisman / Victor Cook

🛡️ Ultimate Spider-Man (2012)

Taking a heavily stylized, comedic approach, this long-running Disney XD series features a Peter Parker who frequently breaks the fourth wall to talk directly to the audience. Recruited by Nick Fury to train under S.H.I.E.L.D., Spider-Man leads a team of teenage heroes while utilizing a massive arsenal of high-tech gadgets and spider-themed vehicles.

👤 Voice Actor: Drake Bell

📅 Era: 2012 - 2017

🎥 Show: Ultimate Spider-Man

🎬 Creative Team: Man of Action

🧪 Marvel's Spider-Man (2017)

Replacing Ultimate Spider-Man in 2017, this series places a heavy narrative emphasis on Peter Parker’s scientific genius and his time attending a prestigious high school for young prodigies. The show heavily features a web of spider-powered allies, including Miles Morales, Spider-Gwen, and Anya Corazon, as they work together to defend New York.

👤 Voice Actor: Robbie Daymond

📅 Era: 2017 - 2020

🎥 Show: Marvel's Spider-Man

🎬 Story Editor: Kevin Shinick

Spidey and His Amazing Friends (2021)

Aimed specifically at preschool audiences, this bright and cheerful Disney Junior show follows a young Peter Parker, Miles Morales, and Gwen Stacy. Together with the help of automated spider-bots, they learn the values of teamwork, friendship, and problem-solving while stopping low-stakes neighborhood antics from classic villains.

👤 Voice Actor: Benjamin Valic

📅 Era: 2021 - Present

🎥 Show: Spidey and His Amazing Friends

🎬 Executive Producer: Harrison Wilcox

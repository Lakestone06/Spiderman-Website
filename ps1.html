📺 PS1 Spider-Man Era

🕷️ Spider-Man (2000)

Released in 2000, Spider-Man is considered one of the most beloved Spider-Man games ever made. Narrated by Stan Lee himself, players swing across rooftops while battling iconic villains such as Venom, Carnage, Doctor Octopus, Mysterio, and Rhino. The game perfectly captured Spider-Man's humor, agility, and acrobatic combat.

⚡ Spider-Man 2: Enter Electro (2001)

Released in 2001, this direct sequel expanded the gameplay and introduced Electro as the main antagonist. Spider-Man must stop Electro from harnessing a powerful energy source that could threaten New York City. The game introduced larger environments and improved combat mechanics.

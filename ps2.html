🏙️ PS2 Spider-Man Era

🎬 Spider-Man: The Movie (2002)

Released in 2002, this game adapted Sam Raimi's first Spider-Man movie starring Tobey Maguire. Players relive Peter Parker's origin story while facing Green Goblin and other villains. The game expanded on events from the movie by adding original missions and new enemies.

🌆 Spider-Man 2 (2004)

Released in 2004, Spider-Man 2 revolutionized superhero gaming by introducing a fully explorable New York City. Players could freely swing between buildings, stop random crimes, and follow the events of the movie. Its realistic web-swinging system is still considered one of the greatest in gaming history.

🎨 Ultimate Spider-Man (2005)

Released in 2005, Ultimate Spider-Man adopted a comic book art style inspired by the Ultimate Marvel universe. Players could control both Spider-Man and Venom, each with unique abilities and missions. The game offered a fresh visual identity while staying faithful to the comics.

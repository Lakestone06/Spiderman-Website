🌌 PS3 Spider-Man Era

🕷️ Spider-Man 3 (2007)

Released in 2007, this game adapted the third Tobey Maguire movie while expanding the open-world experience. Players could switch between the traditional red suit and the powerful black symbiote suit, each offering different abilities.

🌑 Web of Shadows (2008)

Released in 2008, Spider-Man: Web of Shadows presented one of the darkest Spider-Man stories ever created. New York City becomes overrun by alien symbiotes, forcing Spider-Man to make difficult moral choices that directly impact the story's ending.

🌌 Shattered Dimensions (2010)

Released in 2010, this multiverse adventure allowed players to control four different Spider-Men from different universes.

⏳ Edge of Time (2011)

Released in 2011, Spider-Man: Edge of Time focused on Amazing Spider-Man and Spider-Man 2099 working together to save the future.

🕸️ PS4 Spider-Man Era

⚡ The Amazing Spider-Man 2 (2014)

Released in 2014, this open-world game continued Andrew Garfield's Spider-Man story while introducing a hero-or-vigilante reputation system.

📱 Spider-Man Unlimited (2014)

Released in 2014 for mobile devices, Spider-Man Unlimited was an endless runner featuring dozens of Spider-Man variants from across the multiverse.

🏙️ Marvel's Spider-Man (2018)

Released in 2018, Insomniac Games redefined superhero games by creating one of the best Spider-Man experiences ever. Players control an experienced Peter Parker protecting New York City while balancing his personal life.

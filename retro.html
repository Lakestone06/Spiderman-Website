🕹️ Retro Spider-Man Games

🕷️ Spider-Man (1982)

Released in 1982, Spider-Man became the very first video game dedicated to Marvel's iconic superhero. Developed for the Atari 2600, players had to climb skyscrapers while avoiding enemies and defusing bombs placed by the Green Goblin. Although simple by today's standards, the game laid the foundation for decades of Spider-Man adventures and marked the character's debut in the gaming industry.

🧩 Questprobe Featuring Spider-Man (1984)

Released in 1984, this text-based adventure game focused more on solving puzzles than action. Players had to use Spider-Man's abilities intelligently to escape dangerous situations and defeat villains. It was one of the earliest attempts at bringing comic book storytelling into video games.

🔥 Maximum Carnage (1994)

Released in 1994, Maximum Carnage adapted one of Spider-Man's darkest comic storylines. Players fought their way through New York City as Spider-Man and Venom teamed up to stop Carnage and his army of villains. The game became famous for its red cartridge and challenging gameplay.
